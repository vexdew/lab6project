Study

In 2020, I graduated from my school with very good grades in the certificate, which I was very happy about! After graduating from school, I began to think about which university would be best for me to enter. Due to the low level of education in Russia, my family and I decided that I would go to study abroad.

After some time, we learned about Centennial College and began to look for detailed information about it. It turned out that first, it was necessary to gain the required level of knowledge of the English language in one of the language schools, and such a school was the ILSC Toronto Language School.

Almost six months later, I twice reached the maximum level of this language school and received a certificate of successful completion of the ILSC Toronto Language School.

In 2021, I entered Centennial College and studied in a program called Software Engineering Technology. Later, I decided to switch to Seneca College to pursue a Cybersecurity program. After gaining valuable insights and experiences, I made another switch to Durham College, where I am currently studying in the Computer Programming & Analysis program. I am now in the 5th semester of this three-year program, working hard to complete my studies and achieve my goals.
